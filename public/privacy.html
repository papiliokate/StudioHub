Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Oops-Games ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our web games, webviews, and interactive captchas, including "Smack That Captcha".

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe in frictionless and anonymous experiences. We do not ask for, collect, store, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or Reddit account details.

3. Information We Collect Automatically

When you interact with our games, we use industry-standard tools (like Google Analytics) to collect basic, anonymized usage data to help us understand how our games are performing and to improve the user experience. This may include:

  • Device type (e.g., mobile or desktop)
  • Browser type and version
  • General geographical region (country/state, not precise location)
  • Interaction events (e.g., game starts, game completions)

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to personally identify you.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use local storage or session cookies purely for functional purposes (for example, keeping track of your current game streak or ensuring the game runs smoothly). We do not use cross-site tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Reddit community or official social media channels.
